Our Rafting Trips

Choose from our carefully crafted river adventures. Every trip is guided by certified professionals and designed to deliver thrills, safety, and memories that last a lifetime.

Featured Adventures

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Class IV–V  |  Duration: Full Day  |  Min. Age: 16

Translated as "The Devil's Teeth," this legendary run is not for the faint of heart. Expect powerful drops, foaming hydraulics, and tight technical lines through a dramatic canyon gorge. Our expert guides will walk you through every move before you launch — then hold on tight as the river takes over. This is the ultimate rush for experienced paddlers.

Big Mallard

Difficulty: Class III–IV  |  Duration: Half Day  |  Min. Age: 12

Big Mallard is the perfect introduction to serious white water. With consistent wave trains, playful holes, and dramatic splashes that will soak every paddler on board, this trip delivers non-stop excitement for teens and adults alike. It strikes the perfect balance between accessible and genuinely thrilling — a crowd favorite year-round.

Half Day Family Adventure

Difficulty: Class I–II  |  Duration: Half Day  |  Min. Age: 6

This scenic half-day float is designed for families and first-time rafters who want to experience the joy of the river in a relaxed, supportive environment. Glide through gentle rapids, marvel at lush riverside vegetation, and enjoy a mid-trip picnic stop. Our friendly guides make this a safe and memorable outing for the whole family.

Salmon River Run

Difficulty: Class III  |  Duration: Full Day  |  Min. Age: 10

Spend a full day on the stunning Salmon River corridor, winding through lush forest valleys and over a series of exciting Class III rapids. This trip is ideal for groups looking for a mix of paddling action and scenic beauty. Lunch is served riverside, giving you time to soak in the surroundings before the afternoon stretch of the run.

The Gauntlet

Difficulty: Class V  |  Duration: Full Day  |  Min. Age: 18

The Gauntlet is our most extreme and exclusive trip — reserved for experienced paddlers who are ready to test their limits. A relentless chain of Class V rapids demands total focus, physical strength, and complete trust in your team. Limited to groups of six, with a two-to-one guide ratio, this is the pinnacle of white water adventure at Kelechi David Rafting Co.
